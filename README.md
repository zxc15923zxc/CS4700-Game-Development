#  Campfire Survival
*A project for CS4700 – Game Development*

> Keep the fire alive.

---

##  Overview
**Campfire Survival** is a **single player survival game** created for **CS4700: Game Development**.  
Players must gather resources, manage campfire fuel and warmth, and maintain the campfire to survive through the night.  

---

##  Game Features
-  **Campfire Management** — Gather fuel to keep your only light source burning.  
-  **Resource Gathering** — Collect fuels and crafting materials.  
-  **Nighttime Threats** — Beware the darkness; unseen creatures lurk beyond the firelight.  
-  **Stylized Visuals** — Minimalist low-poly art and immersive lighting.  

---

##  How to Play
-  **Move** — WASD
-  **Interact** — E
-  **Open Inventory** — I
